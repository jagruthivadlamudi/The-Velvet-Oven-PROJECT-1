About The Velvet Oven

A boutique bakery café inspired by soft textures, premium ingredients, and warm community energy.

Our Story

The Velvet Oven began as a love for quiet mornings and fresh pastries. We bake daily to keep everything soft, flaky, and rich — the way it should be.

Our Mission

Create a cozy café experience with clean flavors, consistent quality, and a calm atmosphere guests want to return to.

Our Values

Craftsmanship, simplicity, kindness, and attention to detail — in both our baking and our design experience.
